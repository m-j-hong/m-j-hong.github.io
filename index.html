About

I’m MJ, a student at Washington University in St. Louis. I'm majoring in computer science and minoring in both architecture and human-computer interaction. I work in the space where logic and aesthetics meet, designing digital experiences that feel intuitive, elegant, and alive.

I pride myself in crossing disciplines: programming, art, philosophy, psychology, math. To me, beauty and functionality aren’t opposites; they’re the same purpose, expressed through different mediums. I’m fascinated by how design dances between them, making technology feel more human and humans feel more capable.

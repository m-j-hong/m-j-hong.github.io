About

I’m MJ Hong, a computer science student at Washington University in St. Louis, with minors in architecture and human-computer interaction. I work in the space where logic and aesthetics meet, designing digital experiences that feel intuitive, deliberate, and alive.

My background crosses disciplines: code, form, structure, psychology, motion. To me, beauty and functionality aren’t opposites; they’re the same language spoken through different mediums. I’m fascinated by how design can translate between them, making technology feel more human and humans feel more capable.
